Charging Station App

Internship · Delta-N · Low-code

During my internship at Delta-N I noticed there was no easy way for employees to reserve a charging station. So I built one. A low-code app where you can see which stations are available, pick a time slot, and reserve it in seconds. It's been live and in use ever since.

The Problem

Delta-N had charging stations in their parking lot but no system to manage them. People would walk down and find them all taken. No reservations, no overview, no solution. I saw the problem, proposed a fix, and built it.

How It Works

01

Check Availability

Open the app and see which charging stations are free (green) or taken (red) in real time.

02

Pick Your Time

Tap a station, fill in when you need it — date, start time, end time. Simple form, no nonsense.

03

Reserved

One tap on 'reserveer hier' and you're done. The app confirms with a smiley. Honestly pretty satisfying.
